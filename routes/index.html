Hall booking

Use given end points for different operations

1) [POST]create a new Room: /rooms/create

Data to post:

  • seats(String)
  • amenities(Array of Strings)
  • pricePerHour(String)

2) [GET] Get rooms: /rooms

3) [POST] Book a room: /bookings/book

Data to post:

  • customerName(String)
  • Date(String)
  • startTime(String)
  • endTime(String)
  • roomID(_id)

4) [GET] Get bookings: /bookings
